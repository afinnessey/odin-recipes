Baked Mac and Cheese

Description

Description of the dish

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 4 cups sharp cheddar cheese
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook the pasta one minute shy of al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta.
  4. Place pasta in large bowl.
  5. Toss the pasta with olive oil.
  6. Melt butter in sauce pan.
  7. Whisk in flour over medium heat and continue whisking until golden brown.
  8. Whisk in flour over medium heat and continue whisking for about 1 minute until bubbly and golden.
  9. Gradually whisk in the milk and heavy cream until nice and smooth.
  10. Continue whisking until you see bubbles on the surface and then continue cooking and whisking for another 2 minutes.
  11. Whisk in salt and pepper.
  12. Add two cups of shredded cheese and whisk until smooth.
  13. Add another two cups of shredded cheese and continue whisking until creamy and smooth.
  14. Sauce should be nice and thick.
  15. Stir in the cooled pasta until combined and pasta is fully coated with the cheese sauce.
  16. Pour half of the mac and cheese into the prepared baking dish.
  17. Top with remaining 2 cups of shredded cheese and then the remaining mac and cheese.
  18. In a small bowl, combine panko crumbs, Parmesan cheese, melted butter and paprika.
  19. Sprinkle over the top and bake until bubbly and golden brown, about 30 minutes.
  20. Serve immediately.
