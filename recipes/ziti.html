Baked Ziti

Back

Description

Description of the dish

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ziti
  • 4 cups jarred marinara
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • 1 heaping cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmasan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon oil olive

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook the pasta al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta.
  4. Toss the pasta with olive oil.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce in the of bottom of the dish.
  6. Dot the surface of the sauce with half the ricotta cheese.
  7. Ladle a spoonful of sauce into the pasta.
  8. Stir pasta well.
  9. Add the pasta into the casserole dish.
  10. Pour remaining sauce over the pasta.
  11. Dot the remaining ricotta cheese over the pasta.
  12. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmasan on top.
  13. Bake uncovered until the top is lightly browned (about 20-25 minutes).
  14. Let stand for about 10 minutes before serving.
  15. Enjoy!
